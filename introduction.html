Introduction:

W3Schools is a very popular website for learning web development. It has free tutorials, examples, and small exercises that help beginners learn step by step. The explanations are simple, and you can try the code instantly on their online editor. You can learn many topics like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, PHP, and more. Because everything is easy to understand, students and even developers often use W3Schools to quickly learn or revise any topic.

Certificates and assessments:

W3Schools also provides online certificates for different technologies. These certificates can be useful for your resume because they show that you know the subject well. The learning materials are free, but the certification exams are paid. Each exam has quizzes and practical tasks to test your skills. Their clear lessons and interactive examples make it comfortable for anyone to learn and complete the certification at their own speed.
